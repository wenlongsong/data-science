The thesis

Most data teams react to the questions they're handed; I'd rather hunt for the opportunity no one has named yet. And where most leaders ask their team for an answer, I ask mine for an idea — because an answer ends a conversation, but an idea begins the next chapter of growth.

I'm Wenlong Song, and I lead the data science team behind Intuit's Mid-Market business. Across 14+ years, I've used data science to drive growth in consulting, C2C e-commerce, and B2B SaaS — and across Marketing, Sales, and Product. The throughline I keep returning to, and where my energy lives today, is B2B go-to-market and product growth: turning how a business finds, wins, and keeps its customers into something a model can sharpen.

What energizes me most is reinvention — I've led teams through every major shift of the modern data era. In what we deliver: BI and descriptive dashboards → advanced analytics — segmentation, experimentation, causal inference, propensity → LLMs that extract precise insight from unstructured data at scale.

And in how we build it: relational warehouses (SQL Server, Teradata) → Hadoop / Spark → cloud data lakehouses (Snowflake, Databricks) — and from fragmented, manual tooling to end-to-end autonomous workflows powered by Claude Code and MCP. Every shift is a chance to raise what a team believes is possible.
